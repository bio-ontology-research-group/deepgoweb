DeepGOPlus

A convolutional sequence model combined with DIAMOND homology transfer, predicting GO terms across all three sub-ontologies with calibrated scores.

DeepGO-PlusPlus-Light CPU

A CPU-only multi-evidence ensemble — DIAMOND, STRING-Net, a hierarchy-aware CNN, ESM2 embeddings and ProteInfer — with a learned per-aspect integrator and per-component outputs.

API & SPARQL

Call prediction programmatically over a REST API, or query it inline from SPARQL and federate with UniProt — choosing the model and inspecting each component.
